Home

The Home menu helps to show all the recommended, personalized, and latest released tracks. It helps you to have updated and latest chartbusters.

Browse

The Browse menu allows users to have direct access to various languages, genres, upcoming concerts, other contents and categories that are related to music.

Radio

The Radio has some recommendations on radio stations that are based on popular artists and other genres of music.

Your Library

The Your librarysection will auto–personalize based on your listening. There will be different categories like Made for You, Recently Played, Liked Songs.

Tip: Know more about the Your Library.

Albums

Your favorite albums of different artists can be saved in this library.

Adding an album to the library can be done by the following steps:

  1. Open an album that has to be saved in the library.
  2. Click the button.
  3. Click the Save to your library button.

Artists

You can follow your favorite artists to get their songs.

The below steps can help the user to follow the artist:

  1. Search your favorite artist.
  2. Click the Follow button on the page.
The following artist profile will be displayed under the Artists section.

Podcast

You can follow your favorite podcasts by the following steps:

  1. Browse your favorite podcast from the Search bar or Go to Browse.
  2. Click the Follow button on the page.
The following podcast will be displayed under this Podcast section.

Title Ribbon and Search

The Title ribbon provides you the Forward and the Back buttons to do their respective actions.

Also, it provides a search bar that helps to search the songs, podcasts, artists, albums, and other contents easily.

More

The More button has a set of simple options that helps you to access a few other options.
