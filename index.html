Preface

Hi there,

Thanks for your time and the decision you have made to check this portfolio. Nothing but a brief outline about the plans and the process that went through behind making this “Spotify online user’s guide”.

When I thought of making a user’s guide for Spotify, I carefully examined each and every step by from creating a new account to upgrading my account to Premium.

I have roughly designed the user guide flow like how the final version has to be. Then I started documenting it. With implying information mapping, DDLC principles, and by following the MSTP style guides I prepared the entire document.

Initially, I have made the Spotify offline user’s guide using Microsoft Word. I thought the user manual should have all the Spotify elements from the theme, typography, color, font-size, etc. It took a few days to get the offline user guide to finish.

Later, I had a spark that why not I can make this offline to an online user guide.

Again I started with UX research for user’s guide online migration. With having an idea and rough design of UX. I have made wireframes with the help of Balsamiq. By having this wireframe as a low-fidelity design, I transformed it into a proper UX design by using Adobe XD and Figma. Later I prototyped it.

Now, I had to convert the mockups into a real-working website that fits all the devices. With the help of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript I started to code for developing the online version of Spotify’s user’s guide.

Three things I had in my mind during the process of development.

  1. The website has to resemble Spotify’s look and feel.
    For that, I have taken the similar typography of ‘Circular’ which is widely used by Spotify. I have used the color used in Spotify.

  2. The online user guide should follow MSTP style guides.
    For this, I wrote an exclusive CSS3 by integrating all the MSTP standards. With the help of separate classes and Ids, I was able to call the style to the respective .HTML files.

  3. Consistency.
    The entire website should have a consistent style, formatting.

By following the above process, here I’m presenting you Spotify’s online user guide.

My personal note: I may be wrong, I might have made mistakes, I’m open to learn and rectify them. Let me know them through your comments and feedback.

Finally, I again thank you for your patient read.
