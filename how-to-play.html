1. Song info - It shows the details of the song that is played.

2. Slide bar - The Slide bar allows you to go to the desired portion of music.

3. Shuffle - The Shuffle button makes the queued songs play in a shuffling way.

4. Previous - The Previous button in the play ribbon helps you to go to the previous song.

5. Play / Pause - The Play / Pause button helps you to pause or play a song.

6. Next - The Next button helps you to jump to the next song.

7. Repeat - The Repeat button helps you to play the song in a loop.

8. Queue - Click the Queue button to make a song queue next to the current playing.

9. Spotify Connect - Helps your listening to take different devices.
Tip: Know more about Spotify connect.

10. Volume - The Volume slider helps you to control the volume of a song.
